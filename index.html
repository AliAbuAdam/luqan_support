Frequently Asked Questions

iOS

How do I install Luqan on my iPhone?

Visit the App Store, search for "Luqan", and tap "Get" to install. The app requires iOS 13.0 or later.

How do I update Luqan on iOS?

Open the App Store, tap your profile picture, scroll to see pending updates, and tap "Update" next to Luqan. Enable automatic updates in Settings for the latest version.

Android

How do I install Luqan on my Android device?

Visit the Google Play Store, search for "Luqan", and tap "Install". The app requires Android 8.0 or higher.

How do I update Luqan on Android?

Open Google Play Store, tap your profile picture, select "Manage apps & device", find Luqan, and tap "Update". Enable auto-update for automatic updates.
